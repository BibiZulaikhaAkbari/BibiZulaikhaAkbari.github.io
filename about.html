About Me

Who Am I?

I am a 24 years old software developer originally from Afghanistan, currently pursuing my 4th semester in the Software Development program at Mohawk College. My passion for coding has driven me to explore various areas of software development and grows as professional in the field.

What I do?

With a strong foundation in programming languages and software development practices, I enjoy working on a variety of projects, from web development to mobile applications. I am always eager to learn new technologies and tools to enhance my skills and knowledge in the field. My focus is on building user-friendly applications that are efficient and scalable.

My Goals

Coding is more than a skill, it's just my way of expressing creativity and solving problems. I find it energizing and enjoyable to work on projects that require critical thinking and problem-solving skills. My goal is to become a full-stack developer and work on projects that make a positive impact on people's lives. I aim to continuously improve my skills and stay updated with the latest trends in technology.

Beyond Work

When I am not coding or working on projects, I enjoy spending time with my family and friends. I love to travel and explore new places, learn about different cultures, and try new foods. I am also passionate about photography and capturing moments that tell a story. I believe in living life to the fullest and making the most of every opportunity that comes my way.
